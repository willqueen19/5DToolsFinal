About Me

I am currently a fourth year Computer Science and Interactive Media Student at Northeastern University. I chose CS and Interactive media because of my deep interest in creating dramatically better user interfaces and experiences through digital mediums.

I have always been one to dissect the world around me to gain a better understanding of it, and my experience in journalism opened my eyes to the complexities and subtleties of human behavior while using any sort of interface, digital or not. Taking a look at the future, we have only begun to scratch the surface of digital interface design in terms of interface and data visualization. As we move towards a three dimensional digital landscape, I want to create applications that interweave physical and digital reality in ways that improve simplify daily life with little to no effort on the user side.

I am experienced in Java, JavaScript, HTML and CSS, React Native, React.js, and Redux. I am also certified in SolidWorks.
